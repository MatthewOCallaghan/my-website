About

Meet Matthew

I'm a Software Developer with strong frontend and backend experience and a passion for UX design. Until a recent working holiday in New Zealand where I contracted remotely, I spent over four years at Videoloft, a cloud provider for video surveillance systems. There, I rebuilt the web frontend from the ground up, consolidating multiple codebases into a single React application. As the company scaled, I later worked alongside other developers to maintain and extend this app, while also collaborating with the CPO and external design agencies to produce agreed specifications and translate them into functional user interfaces.

I'm a details person who takes pride in delivering polished user experiences and writing clean, maintainable code, whilst balancing the pace of a startup environment.

My personal projects include Experimental Gravity, an iOS puzzle game built in Unity with C#, which is available on the App Store. I also design, develop and maintain the website of local business Cakes by Katie, which serves as their primary customer acquisition channel and has contributed significantly to their early success.

In my academic studies, I graduated with a first-class degree in Computer Science from the University of Southampton, having achieved straight A*s at A-Level and GCSE. My latest project, Atomic Exams, is a Next.js web app designed to help GCSE and A-Level students study more strategically.
