Cakes by Katie

Personal website for an aspiring cake decorator to display her work and gain clients for new projects to practise her skills.

View website

Tell me more ...

Cakes by Katie seeks to showcase the work of an up-and-coming cake artist and appeal to potential clients who would like a high-quality cake at cost price. This website aims to provide her with a more professional web presence than just her Instagram account as she embarks on her new career. A light colour scheme and spacious layout creates a feel of elegance and quality, whilst cursive fonts are used to give it a more personal touch.
